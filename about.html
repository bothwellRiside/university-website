Liverpool University of Distance Learning

About the University

Liverpool University of Distance Learning is a higher education institution based in Liverpool, United Kingdom, offering both on-campus and distance learning programmes designed to meet the needs of a diverse, international student body.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide accessible, flexible, and academically sound education that prepares students for professional practice, further study, and lifelong learning.

Modes of Study

The University offers both on-campus and online study options, ensuring flexibility while maintaining consistent academic standards across all programmes.

Academic Approach

Teaching and assessment are guided by principles of academic integrity, critical inquiry, and structured learning outcomes aligned with higher education expectations.

Our Students

Our student community includes local learners, international students, and working professionals seeking flexible, UK-based higher education opportunities.
